Tutorial

Fit a linear model use mtcars dataset:

# Fit a linear model
model <- lm(mpg ~ wt + hp + qsec + am, data = mtcars)

Find SSE of the linear model:

obtain_SSE(model)
## [1] 160.0665

Find coefficients standard errors of the linear model:

obtain_se(model)
## (Intercept)          wt          hp        qsec          am 
##   9.3188688   0.8898986   0.0141506   0.4388703   1.3971471

Find t-statistic of the linear model:

obtain_t_stats(model)
## (Intercept)          wt          hp        qsec          am 
##    1.871492   -3.638726   -1.247052    1.847021    2.093913

Find p values of each t-statistic for the linear model:

obtain_p_value(model)
## (Intercept)          wt          hp        qsec          am 
## 0.072149342 0.001141407 0.223087932 0.075731202 0.045790788

Find residual standard error and degrees of freedom:

obtain_residual_info(model)
## $residual_se
## [1] 2.434828
## 
## $df_resi
## [1] 27

Find R-square and adjusted R-square:

obtain_r_square(model)
## $r_square
## [1] 0.857851
## 
## $adj_r_square
## [1] 0.8367919

Find F statistic and its p-value of the linear model:

obtain_f_stats(model)
## $f_statistic
## [1] 40.73539

## $df_model
## [1] 4

## $df_resi
